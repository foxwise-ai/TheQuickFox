Welcome to TheQuickFox

You're about to make your life easier in unexpected ways.

Let's say you get a support request:

PretendSupport
buzz@killington.com:
Cancel my account
  1. With Reply field focused, press ⌃ control ×2
  2. That activates TheQuickFox. In TheQuickFox reply to the customer (e.g.: "Just cancelled. Feedback?") and press Enter.
  3. TheQuickFox takes your terse response that you'd never send to a customer and makes it professional, friendly, or even flirty!

Let's say you want feedback on your new logo.

Pretend Logo Maker
  1. Press ⌃ control ×2
  2. When TheQuickFox pops up, type: "Give me feedback on my logo" then press Enter.
